# open

Shows the `<Calendar />` on initial load.

> #### Note:
>
> - By default, either the selected date or first selectable date recieves `:focus` when the `<Calendar />` is opened.
> - If the `open` prop **_is_** used, this `:focus` functionality is disabled until the `<Calendar />` is interacted with or closed.
